Press a Key to Start

  • Simon Starts the Show: Watch as Simon lights up and plays sounds for one colors. Pay close attention!

  • Your Turn to Shine: Once Simon finishes, it's your turn to repeat the colors. Press the buttons matching the same colors and order you saw them in.

  • Did You Get It Right?: If you pressed the buttons in the right order, Simon plays a happy tune and shows you a new, longer sequence! You're getting good!

  • Uh Oh, a Slip-Up: If you press the wrong button or forget the order, Simon lets you know with a different sound and the game ends. But don't worry, you can always try again!
